About

Who is Jeff Cobb? I'm from Jacksonville NC, I have 7 children and 1 grandchild. I have had a lot of titles in my life-time. I started my long journey in 1990, after spending several years trying to figure this thing called "life" out, I entered the workforce. I have been a police officer, armoured crewman, restaurant manager and teacher. In 2000 I entered the teaching/coaching profession that has lasted the last 20 plus years. As a teacher I had the pleasure to teach in many different areas, business, technology, digital arts, engineering and my favorite, computer science. While completing my masters degree I was introduced to Web Design, and the passion began. The last few years of my profession I spent teaching young adults the joy of coding, using different platforms to guide them on their journey.

After retiring as a teacher in 2020 is stumbled around trying to figure out what was the next chapter. That brought me to believe what my father once told me. "IF you like your job you never work a day of your life". I thought that it was time to follow what has become a passion, Web design. I entered into Treehouse's Front End Web Development Techdegree and have aspirations of becoming a Front-End-Web Developer. I realize that it won't be easy and that I have much more to learn, but I have never turned down a good challenge. Thanks for visiting my site and please feel free to contact me with any questions.
